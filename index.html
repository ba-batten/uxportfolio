About Me

emailba_batten@live.com brandonbatten

I enjoy being creative, solving problems, and cool tech. Creating something great from simply an idea gives me a rush. I built my first website as part of my senior project in high school. Looking back, it was a rough site, but I learned a lot. I was introduced to HTML and how to publish a site. I later studied to be a graphic designer in college and designed a few more websites for various projects.

A lot has changed since I first built that website. I enjoyed a career in finance but was never passionate about it. I harkened back to that website I built years ago and my design background. I focused on improving my web development skills and was ultimately able to combine my design and development background into a UX design career with one of the largest credit unions in the world.

My design philosophy is centered around those who will be using what I design. If I fail to make users the focus, then my designs will ultimately fail. Users should be involved from the very start of a new project.

I welcome new challenges and the opportunity to grow professionally and personally.
